Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new note?

To create a new note, simply enter a title and your content in the provided fields and click "Save Note".

Can I edit my notes?

Yes, you can edit your notes by clicking on the note title in the sidebar, making changes, and clicking "Save Note".

How do I delete a note?

You can delete a note by clicking the "Trash" button next to the note in the sidebar. Deleted notes will move to the Trash section.

Can I recover deleted notes?

Yes, you can recover deleted notes from the Trash section. Notes in the Trash can be restored or permanently deleted.
