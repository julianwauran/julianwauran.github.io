About This Application

Welcome to my note-taking application. My name is Julian. My goal is to provide a simple yet powerful tool to help you organize your thoughts, tasks, and ideas efficiently.

Features

  • Create Notes: Easily create and manage your notes with a clean interface.
  • Save and Edit: Save notes and make changes anytime you need.
  • Trash and Restore: Move notes to the trash and restore them if needed. Notes are automatically deleted after 30 days of being in the trash.
